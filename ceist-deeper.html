What it carries

In Vedic astrology the Moon is the mind and the heart — how you feel your way through the world. Yours sits exalted in Taurus, its strongest seat, in Rohini: the nakshatra of growth, beauty, and rootedness.

This is a steady, nourishing emotional nature. You attach deeply to home and to what you build, and you grow things slowly — relationships, work, a sense of place. The 5th house places that warmth in creativity and in what you love.

In timing, this tends toward
  • Favouring patience and slow consolidation over sudden leaps
  • Decisions that protect home, roots, and what's already growing
  • A pull to nurture rather than uproot — worth noticing when restlessness mimics readiness
