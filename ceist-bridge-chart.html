✦ Where both charts speak together
Saturn shapes you. Vedic places Saturn in your Lagna (H1 in Sagittarius). Western places it in Capricorn — its own sign, powerful, in H1. Both say: you build slowly, earn through discipline, and your path is earned rather than given.
Mercury in Aquarius — in both charts. Your Mercury does not shift sign across systems. Original thinking, systems, categories: this is not a trait, it's a frequency.
Ideas are your engine. Western: Mars + Jupiter conjunct in Gemini H5. Vedic: Mars + Moon + Jupiter in Taurus H6. Both systems load Gemini-adjacent energy with your drive. You move through language and learning.
Venus breaks free. Vedic: Venus in Aquarius (unconventional love). Western: Venus in Aries (direct, impulsive love). Both say you don't love quietly or conventionally.
"Vedic astrology gives you your karma. Western gives you your psychology. Together, they give you yourself."
